    prep time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:
    chickpeas                     1 can
    chili powder                  0.5 tsp
    crushed red pepper            1 dash
    garlic powder                 1 tsp
    ground black pepper           1 pinch
    ground cumin                  1 tbsp
    olive oil                     2 tbsp
    sea salt                      1 pinch

Cookware:
    small bowl
    baking sheet

Steps:
     1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
        [–]
     2. Whisk olive oil, ground cumin, garlic powder, chili powder, sea salt, ground black pepper, and
        crushed red pepper together in a small bowl. Add chickpeas (drained and rinsed) and toss to coat.
        Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet.
        [chickpeas: 1 can; chili powder: 0.5 tsp; crushed red pepper: 1 dash; garlic powder: 1 tsp; ground
        black pepper: 1 pinch; ground cumin: 1 tbsp; olive oil: 2 tbsp; sea salt: 1 pinch]
     3. Roast in the oven, stirring occasionally, until nicely browned and slighly crispy, about 45
        minutes.
        [–]
